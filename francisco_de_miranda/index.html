Francisco de Miranda

Tyranny cannot reign but on the ignorance of the people

El criollo universal

Basic information

Full name: Sebastián Francisco de Miranda y Rodríguez
Born: March 28, 1750. Caracas, Venezuela
Die: July 14, 1816. San Fernando, Spain
Fight in: United States independence, France revolution, Venezuela independence
Nicknames: El criollo universal, El Precursor

Time line

1750

March 28, was born in Caracas, Venezuela, part of a family from the same country.

1762

January 10, started his studies in the Caraca's University. There he learned latin, grammar, history, religion, arithmetic, geography, logic, phisics and metaphysics. Some people said he got the medicine title.

1762
1771

January 25, his father decided to send him to Spain to serve the Real Spanish Army. That's how he left Venezuela on board of the Prince Federico frigate; started a long period of travels.

March 27, arrive to Madrid and began a whole new process in his education, where thanks to the open world of the European continent, he had the oppotunity to learn from the literature of Maquiavelo, Julio Cesar, Burlamaqui, Pope, Virgilio and others. He began to assemble his personal library; One of the greatest of the time, contained books that the inquiry considered as forbidden.

Pursuing the willing of being part of the Spanish army, started the studies of the military arts.

1773

January 3, after two hard and long years studying the military arts he obtained the title of Captain of the Spanish Army.

1773
The travels...
1775

In Africa, gets involved in the defense of Melilla, fighting against the Morocco's Sultan.

1783

June 1, arrives to the United States where spent 18 months. Studied all the North American process and meets people as Jorge Washington, Alexander Hamilton, Thomas Paine and Gilbert M. de La Fayette. Here is where he start to plan all the Hispanic America Independece Project.

1783
1785

Starts a long travel in Europe. Visited Netherlands, Prussia, almost all Italy, Germany, France, Switzerland, Belgium and Greece.

1787

Meets Catherine the Great. She authorizes him to use the russian military uniform. Some people say they were lovers.

1787
1792

August 25, gets the title of Field Marshal of the French Revolutionary Army.

The first try...
1806

February 2, parts to Haití to start a revolution in the continent.

March 12, Miranda creates the tricolor flag (yellow, blue and red). This flag is used in our days by Colombia, Ecuador and Venezuela

August 3, arrives to the city of Coro, Venezuela, hoist the flag, but without the support of the people from the city, and with the threat of the Spain army, he is forced to leave and return to England.

1806
The second one...
1810

October 10, after a conversation with Simón Bolivar, Andrés Bello and Luis López Méndez in England, he decides to come back to Venezuela where is named lieutenant general of the army.

1812

Due to the defeat of Simón Bolivar by Monteverde in Puerto Cabello and the earthquake (March 26), Miranda started some negotiations with Spain which led to the lost of the First Republic.

In the night between the 30 and 31 of July, is putted under arrest by Bolivar under the charges of treason. Bolivar gave him to the Spanish.

1812
1814

Miranda is under arrest in La Carraca, Spain.

1816

July 14, under arrest and betrayed by his friend, died, the first Universal venezuelan.

1816
